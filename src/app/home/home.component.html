About Us

Our BST Blood, Sweat & Tears blood bank was founded 50 years ago. We have always aimed to keep pace with the new technologies serving blood transfusions. We have been accredited by the American Association of Blood Banks (AABB) and the Foundation for the Accreditation for Cellular Therapy (FACT). Our technicians, nurses and medical staff are a family who put the needs of the patient and the donor first and foremost.

Why donate?

By donating blood at Blood, Sweat & Tears blood bank, you can help save lives with your blood. Hospital patients need about 44,000 blood donations DAILY and about one of every seven people who go to a hospital end up needing donated blood as part of their treatment.

Donations of more rare blood types are especially in demand. For example, people with O negative blood only make up about 8 percent of the population, but their blood can be given to patients of all blood types, making their donors highly needed.

Blood transfusions are needed for: women with complications of pregnancy, children with severe anaemia, people with severe trauma, and for cancer patients. It's also needed for regular transfusions for people with conditions such as thalassaemia and sickle cell disease.

who can donate?

Most people can give blood. You can give blood if you:

  • are fit and healthy
  • weigh between 7 stone 12 lbs and 25 stone, or 50kg and 160kg
  • are aged between 17 and 66 (or 70 if you have given blood before)
  • are over 70 and have given blood in the last two years
